About Shiny Windows

Thanks for checking out Shiny Windows! Brian Bondietti is the owner and he has 14 years experience in the window cleaning business. We specialize in 'nose-to-glass' window cleaning which means we try to reach any window by ladder first if possible in order to ensure a high level of detail, if a window is not able to be safely reached, we use the newest technology to clean your windows from the ground with Waterfed poles and pure water technology. Pure water cleaning works just like the spotless car rinse at the car wash and can be utilized whenever safety is a concern over perfectionism.
